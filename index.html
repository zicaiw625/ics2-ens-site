ICS EU ENS HS6 Prep

Google Sheets add-on for preparing EU ICS2 ENS pre-arrival data with HS6.

Purpose

Help shippers, forwarders, and traders prepare accurate EU ICS2 Entry Summary (ENS) data directly in Google Sheets. The add-on structures required fields, validates HS6 and descriptions, and exports carrier-neutral CSV for submission via your carrier or customs platform.

What it does

  • Create ENS templates (Master/House) for sea, road, and rail.
  • Validate HS6 format (6 digits), goods description quality, UN/LOCODE shape, and structured party addresses.
  • Generate a validation log and a completeness score within the same file.
  • Export Master/House CSV for upload to your chosen system.

How it works

  • Open a spreadsheet → Extensions → Add-ons → ICS EU ENS HS6 Prep.
  • Generate templates → fill data → run “Validate”.
  • Download CSV and submit through your carrier/platform.

Data access & permissions

Operates only in the spreadsheet where it is installed. No developer servers are used.

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets.currentonly – read/write the current file to create templates, validations, logs, and exports.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui – display the sidebar/dialog inside Google Sheets.

No access to other Drive files, Gmail, or external accounts.

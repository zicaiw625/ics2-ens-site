Privacy Policy

Effective: 10 September 2025

Overview

ICS2 ENS Builder for Google Sheets (“the Add-on”) is published by Your Company (“we”, “us”). This policy explains how we process data when you use the Add-on.

What data we process

  • Spreadsheet content you select or edit in the current Google Sheet (e.g., ENS fields such as parties, HS codes, weights). Used to validate and generate exports.
  • Generated files (CSV/XML) we create in your Google Drive via the drive.file scope.
  • Operational metadata such as timestamps of “Validate” and “Export” actions written to an in-sheet log.

What we do not collect

  • We do not transmit your shipment data to our servers.
  • We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not use your data to train generalized AI/ML models.

Purposes of processing

  • Provide core functionality: template creation, validation, and export.
  • Improve reliability and user experience (e.g., anonymized error counts if you opt in; off by default).

Legal basis

We process data at your direction to perform the Add-on’s functions you request (contractual necessity / performance of a service you initiate).

Google API Services User Data Policy

Where we use Google APIs, we adhere to Google’s Limited Use requirements. Data obtained via Google APIs is used only to provide or improve user-facing features, is not sold or transferred except as necessary to provide or improve the Add-on, and is not used for serving ads.

Data storage & retention

  • Validation runs locally in your browser session and Apps Script context.
  • CSV/XML exports are stored in your Google Drive under your account. You control retention and deletion.
  • We do not retain your ENS content on our servers.

Third parties

We use Google Workspace/Apps Script to run the Add-on. If you enable optional remote XSD validation (if offered in future), XML may be sent to a validation endpoint strictly for schema checking without persistent storage; details would be documented and disabled by default.

Security

We follow industry best practices for access minimization (least-privilege scopes) and rely on Google’s authentication and authorization flows.

Your choices

  • You can revoke add-on access in your Google Account permissions.
  • You may delete generated files from your Google Drive at any time.

Children’s privacy

The Add-on is not directed to children. Do not use it if you are under the age required by local law to consent to data processing.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will post changes here with a new “Effective” date.

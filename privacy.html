Privacy Policy

Product: ICS2 Basic – Google Sheets Editor Add-on
Controller: ensworks
Effective date: 2025-09-21

1. Overview

This policy explains what data the add-on accesses, why, how it is used, and your choices.

2. Scope

This policy covers the ICS2 Basic add-on used inside Google Sheets. It does not cover third-party sites linked from our website.

3. Google User Data We Access

OAuth scopes requested

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui – to display and run HtmlService sidebars and dialogs within Google Sheets.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets.currentonly – to read/write only the spreadsheet where the add-on is installed.

Categories of data (current spreadsheet only)

  • Sheet names, headers, cell values you provide, data validation/formatting.
  • Add-on output written to the same file (e.g., ICS2_Master, ICS2_House, ICS2_ValidationLog, ICS2_Score).

We do not access: other Drive files, Gmail, Contacts, Calendar, or any external accounts.

4. How We Use the Data

  • Create templates and validations in the current spreadsheet.
  • Run on-sheet checks and write results to a log/score sheet in the same file.
  • Generate CSV for download in your browser when you click export.
  • No transmission to our servers; no advertising or profiling.

5. Processing, Storage, and Retention

  • Processing occurs within Google Apps Script and your browser.
  • We do not store spreadsheet data on developer or third-party servers.
  • All outputs remain in your spreadsheet. You control retention by editing or deleting those sheets.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell or share Google user data with third parties.
  • Data may be disclosed only if required by law.

7. Security

  • Least-privilege design: currentonly scope and @OnlyCurrentDoc limit access to the open file.
  • Transport security provided by Google’s infrastructure.
  • No custom external data stores or telemetry are used.

8. Your Choices

  • Uninstall via Extensions → Add-ons → Manage add-ons.
  • Revoke access at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Delete any ICS2_* sheets or the entire spreadsheet to remove add-on outputs.

9. Children

The add-on is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from them.

10. International Transfers

No developer-hosted transfers occur. If this changes, we will update this policy before enabling such features.

11. Sub-processors

None. The add-on does not use third-party processors for your Google user data.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The “Effective date” will reflect the latest version.

Privacy Policy

This Google Sheets add-on runs within your Google account context:

  1. Reads and writes only the current spreadsheet (spreadsheets.currentonly).
  2. Creates and manages files it generates in your Google Drive (drive.file).
  3. No ENS data is sent to our servers; we do not store, transmit, or sell your data.
  4. We comply with Google API Services User Data Policy (Limited Use).
